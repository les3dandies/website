Company, Process & Delivery

Les3Dandies presents a new culinary experience which awakens all the senses.

Les3Dandies are Thibaut, Olivier and Edouard. As young epicureans, we met each other whilst studying at the renowned SKEMA Business School. Our passion for gourmet food and enthusiasm regarding new technologies encouraged us to collaborate in creating this company. From its early beginnings as just an initial idea, to the acquisition of the required machinery and sourcing of a master chocolatier - Les3Dandies was born after months of dedicated work. The goal has always been to create something that is not only delicious but also visually spectacular. This goal is now a reality and we are proud to be able to share this chocolate revolution with you.

With the expertise of our master chocolatier and 3D printing technology, Les3Dandies can create customised 3D chocolate designs. These creations offer a unique alternative to generic chocolate figurines on the market. Using only the finest quality chocolate, we are able to tailor-make any shape you may request whether it be a chocolate replica of a loved one or a box of branded chocolates. Aside from specialising in 3D creations, we also offer customised 2D chocolates and an array of hampers including other gourmet food goods. Hampers by Les3Dandies bring together other local artisanal gourmet goods - these complement the exquisite taste of our chocolate while still showcasing the 3D chocolate design as the main feature. These creations offer a new experiential way to enjoy chocolate - savour the taste, delight in the harmony of the blended aromas, and marvel at the minute detail in the design.

Les 3Dandies melts together the tradition of French chocolatiers with the creative possibilities that 3D technology offers. The chocolate is an original recipe of the finest quality and always sourced ethically. We offer a large range of products in an array of flavours and shapes. Our master chocolatier melts down our original chocolate and lovingly pours it into our modelling machine. Under the his eyes, the chocolate will slowly drip down to form any desired shape. The shape formed by the modelling machine is determined by the uploaded 3D file that we receive or as conceived by our inhouse designer. Once the chocolate has cooled - we are ready to wrap and send with love to you.

As well as offering customised creations, we also have an original range of creations designed in collaboration with local artists. This collection is curated by Jordi Fornes. These artistic creations are ready for immediate order.

Our chocolate is sourced fairtrade. Our production methods are designed so as to have zero wastage. The 3D chocolate technology doesn’t waste any chocolate mix. The machines are designed so as to use only the exact quantity of chocolate needed. The packaging we use is not only beautiful but made from recycled materials. To strengthen our environmental mission, we will deliver our products by bicycle service within Paris city centre. As well as committing to environmental sustainability, we hope to give back to our local community. We will source our materials and additional produce from local suppliers where possible to ensure a truly French product. We will also contribute to sustainable development in West Africa by donating a percentage of our profits to education and training for cocoa farmers to help them to increase their yields and profits.

The delivery service we offer is of a premium level. We seek to ensure that the product is not only of the highest standard but its packaging and delivery are too. Our delivery service operates meticulously an efficient service with friendly couriers arriving to your door by trendy bicycle. Les3Dandies will ensure that your orders are delivered on time, in perfect condition and with their temperature at all times moderated.

Currently we are only operating within the Paris area. Shortly we will be expanding operations to include delivery in greater France and overseas.
